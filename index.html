I am currently a PhD candidate under supervision of Prof. Rongchao Jin at the Department of Chemistry of Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). My research interests mainly focus on designing and synthesizing atomically precise metal nanoclusters and their application in catalysis.

Before my PhD, I got my BE (Bachelor of Engineering) and ME (Master of Engineering) degree in Central South University under supervision of Prof. Jun Liu, working on the design and synthesis of electrode materials for high-performance Lithium/Sodium-ion batteries.

I am also interested in Machine learning techniques. One of my research plan is combining machine learning with traditional chemistry, looking for potential materials for various application, such as electrochemical catalytic materials.
